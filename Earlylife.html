Early Life

I am Mahjabin Imran, almost fifteen years old, born on July 7, 2006, in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
I have two younger siblings. I came to Canada in 2018. I would describe myself as ambitious,
messy, hard-headed a bit egoistic. If we talk about plans for the future, I plan on investing
in either the stock market or real estate or maybe both. For now, I am focusing on getting good
grades. So, I have a chance to go to a good university. Besides academics, I am a dancer even
though I haven't performed in almost two years. I started dancing when I was three. I learnt
traditional Kathak(a dance form) and Folk(a dance form) of our country, Mix of Hip-hop and
Contemporary. I also did Martial arts, Karate to be precise. I am also a self-taught artist.
